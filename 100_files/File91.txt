<p><b>August 29</b> is the 241st day of the year (242nd in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 124 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Monday, Wednesday or Saturday (58 in 400 years each) than on Thursday or Friday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Tuesday or Sunday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=August_29&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/708" title="708">708</a> – <a href="/wiki/Wad%C5%8Dkaichin" title="Wadōkaichin">Copper coins</a> are minted in Japan for the first time (Traditional <a href="/wiki/Japanese_calendar" title="Japanese calendar">Japanese date</a>: August 10, 708).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1261" title="1261">1261</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Urban_IV" title="Pope Urban IV">Pope Urban IV</a> succeeds <a href="/wiki/Pope_Alexander_IV" title="Pope Alexander IV">Pope Alexander IV</a> as the 182nd pope.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1315" title="1315">1315</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Montecatini" title="Battle of Montecatini">Battle of Montecatini</a>: The army of the <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Pisa" title="Republic of Pisa">Republic of Pisa</a>, commanded by <a href="/wiki/Uguccione_della_Faggiuola" title="Uguccione della Faggiuola">Uguccione della Faggiuola</a>, wins a decisive victory against the joint forces of the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Naples" title="Kingdom of Naples">Kingdom of Naples</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Florence" title="Republic of Florence">Republic of Florence</a> despite being outnumbered.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1350" title="1350">1350</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Winchelsea" title="Battle of Winchelsea">Battle of Winchelsea</a> (or <a href="/wiki/Les_Espagnols_sur_Mer" class="mw-redirect" title="Les Espagnols sur Mer">Les Espagnols sur Mer</a>): The <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Naval_fleet" title="Naval fleet">naval fleet</a> under King <a href="/wiki/Edward_III_of_England" title="Edward III of England">Edward III</a> defeats a <a href="/wiki/Crown_of_Castile" title="Crown of Castile">Castilian</a> fleet of 40 ships.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1475" title="1475">1475</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Picquigny" title="Treaty of Picquigny">Treaty of Picquigny</a> ends a brief war between the kingdoms of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_France" title="Kingdom of France">France</a> and England.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1484" title="1484">1484</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Innocent_VIII" title="Pope Innocent VIII">Pope Innocent VIII</a> succeeds <a href="/wiki/Pope_Sixtus_IV" title="Pope Sixtus IV">Pope Sixtus IV</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1498" title="1498">1498</a> – <a href="/wiki/Vasco_da_Gama" title="Vasco da Gama">Vasco da Gama</a> decides to depart <a href="/wiki/Kozhikode" title="Kozhikode">Calicut</a> and return to <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Portugal" title="Kingdom of Portugal">Kingdom of Portugal</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1521" title="1521">1521</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Turks" class="mw-redirect" title="Ottoman Turks">Ottoman Turks</a> capture <a href="/wiki/N%C3%A1ndorfeh%C3%A9rv%C3%A1r" class="mw-redirect" title="Nándorfehérvár">Nándorfehérvár</a> (Belgrade).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1526" title="1526">1526</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Moh%C3%A1cs" title="Battle of Mohács">Battle of Mohács</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Turks" class="mw-redirect" title="Ottoman Turks">Ottoman Turks</a> led by <a href="/wiki/Suleiman_the_Magnificent" title="Suleiman the Magnificent">Suleiman the Magnificent</a> defeat and kill the last <a href="/wiki/Jagiellonian" class="mw-redirect" title="Jagiellonian">Jagiellonian</a> king of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Hungary" title="Kingdom of Hungary">Hungary</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Bohemia" title="Kingdom of Bohemia">Bohemia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1541" title="1541">1541</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Turks" class="mw-redirect" title="Ottoman Turks">Ottoman Turks</a> capture <a href="/wiki/Buda" title="Buda">Buda</a>, the capital of the <a href="/wiki/Hungarian_Kingdom" class="mw-redirect" title="Hungarian Kingdom">Hungarian Kingdom</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1728" title="1728">1728</a> – The city of <a href="/wiki/Nuuk" title="Nuuk">Nuuk</a> in <a href="/wiki/Greenland" title="Greenland">Greenland</a> is founded as the fort of Godt-Haab by the <a href="/wiki/List_of_governors_of_Greenland" title="List of governors of Greenland">royal governor</a> <a href="/wiki/Claus_Paarss" title="Claus Paarss">Claus Paarss</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1756" title="1756">1756</a> – <a href="/wiki/Frederick_the_Great" title="Frederick the Great">Frederick the Great</a> attacks <a href="/wiki/Electorate_of_Saxony" title="Electorate of Saxony">Saxony</a>, beginning the <a href="/wiki/Seven_Years%27_War" title="Seven Years' War">Seven Years' War</a> in Europe.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1758" title="1758">1758</a> – The first American <a href="/wiki/Indian_reservation" title="Indian reservation">Indian reservation</a> is established, at Indian Mills, New Jersey.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1778" title="1778">1778</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Revolutionary_War" title="American Revolutionary War">American Revolutionary War</a>: British and American forces battle indecisively at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Rhode_Island" title="Battle of Rhode Island">Battle of Rhode Island</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1786" title="1786">1786</a> – <a href="/wiki/Shays%27_Rebellion" title="Shays' Rebellion">Shays' Rebellion</a>, an armed uprising of <a href="/wiki/Massachusetts" title="Massachusetts">Massachusetts</a> farmers, begins in response to high debt and tax burdens.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1807" title="1807">1807</a> – British troops under Sir <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Wellesley,_1st_Duke_of_Wellington" title="Arthur Wellesley, 1st Duke of Wellington">Arthur Wellesley</a> defeat a Danish militia outside <a href="/wiki/Copenhagen" title="Copenhagen">Copenhagen</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_K%C3%B8ge" title="Battle of Køge">Battle of Køge</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1825" title="1825">1825</a> – <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Portugal" title="Kingdom of Portugal">Kingdom of Portugal</a> recognizes the Independence of <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1831" title="1831">1831</a> – <a href="/wiki/Michael_Faraday" title="Michael Faraday">Michael Faraday</a> discovers <a href="/wiki/Electromagnetic_induction" title="Electromagnetic induction">electromagnetic induction</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1842" title="1842">1842</a> – <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Nanking" title="Treaty of Nanking">Treaty of Nanking</a> signing ends the <a href="/wiki/First_Opium_War" title="First Opium War">First Opium War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1861" title="1861">1861</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Civil_War" title="American Civil War">American Civil War</a>: <a href="/wiki/United_States_Navy" title="United States Navy">United States Navy</a> squadron captures forts at Hatteras Inlet, <a href="/wiki/North_Carolina" title="North Carolina">North Carolina</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1869" title="1869">1869</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Mount_Washington_Cog_Railway" title="Mount Washington Cog Railway">Mount Washington Cog Railway</a> opens, making it the world's first mountain-climbing <a href="/wiki/Rack_railway" title="Rack railway">rack railway</a>.</li>

<p><b>April 25</b> is the 115th day of the year (116th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 250 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Monday, Wednesday or Saturday (58 in 400 years each) than on Thursday or Friday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Tuesday or Sunday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=April_25&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/404_BC" title="404 BC">404 BC</a> – <a href="/wiki/Peloponnesian_War" title="Peloponnesian War">Peloponnesian War</a>: <a href="/wiki/Lysander" title="Lysander">Lysander</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sparta" title="Sparta">Spartan</a> armies defeated the <a href="/wiki/Classical_Athens" title="Classical Athens">Athenians</a> and the war ends.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/775" title="775">775</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Bagrevand" title="Battle of Bagrevand">Battle of Bagrevand</a> puts an end to an <a href="/wiki/Armenians" title="Armenians">Armenian</a> rebellion against the <a href="/wiki/Abbasid_Caliphate" title="Abbasid Caliphate">Abbasid Caliphate</a>. Muslim control over <a href="/wiki/Transcaucasia" title="Transcaucasia">Transcaucasia</a> is solidified and its Islamization begins, while several major Armenian <i><a href="/wiki/Nakharar" title="Nakharar">nakharar</a></i> families lose power and their remnants flee to the <a href="/wiki/Byzantine_Empire" title="Byzantine Empire">Byzantine Empire</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/799" title="799">799</a> – After mistreatment and <a href="/wiki/Disfigurement" title="Disfigurement">disfigurement</a> by the citizens of Rome, pope <a href="/wiki/Pope_Leo_III" title="Pope Leo III">Leo III</a> flees to the <a href="/wiki/Francia" title="Francia">Frankish</a> court of king <a href="/wiki/Charlemagne" title="Charlemagne">Charlemagne</a> at <a href="/wiki/Paderborn" title="Paderborn">Paderborn</a> for protection.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1134" title="1134">1134</a> – The name <a href="/wiki/Zagreb" title="Zagreb">Zagreb</a> was mentioned for the first time in the Felician Charter relating to the establishment of the Zagreb Bishopric around 1094.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1607" title="1607">1607</a> – <a href="/wiki/Eighty_Years%27_War" title="Eighty Years' War">Eighty Years' War</a>: The Dutch fleet <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Gibraltar_(1607)" title="Battle of Gibraltar (1607)">destroys</a> the anchored Spanish fleet at <a href="/wiki/Gibraltar" title="Gibraltar">Gibraltar</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1644" title="1644">1644</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Chongzhen_Emperor" title="Chongzhen Emperor">Chongzhen Emperor</a>, the last Emperor of <a href="/wiki/Ming_dynasty" title="Ming dynasty">Ming dynasty</a> China, commits suicide during a peasant rebellion led by <a href="/wiki/Li_Zicheng" title="Li Zicheng">Li Zicheng</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1707" title="1707">1707</a> – A coalition of England, the Netherlands and Portugal is defeated by a Franco-Spanish army at <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Almansa" title="Battle of Almansa">Almansa</a> (Spain) in the <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Spanish_Succession" title="War of the Spanish Succession">War of the Spanish Succession</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1792" title="1792">1792</a> – <a href="/wiki/Highwayman" title="Highwayman">Highwayman</a> <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Jacques_Pelletier" title="Nicolas Jacques Pelletier">Nicolas J. Pelletier</a> becomes the first person executed by <a href="/wiki/Guillotine" title="Guillotine">guillotine</a>.</li>
<li>1792 – "<a href="/wiki/La_Marseillaise" title="La Marseillaise">La Marseillaise</a>" (the French <a href="/wiki/National_anthem" title="National anthem">national anthem</a>) is composed by <a href="/wiki/Claude_Joseph_Rouget_de_Lisle" title="Claude Joseph Rouget de Lisle">Claude Joseph Rouget de Lisle</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1804" title="1804">1804</a> – The western Georgian <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Imereti" title="Kingdom of Imereti">kingdom of Imereti</a> accepts the <a href="/wiki/Suzerainty" title="Suzerainty">suzerainty</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Russian_Empire" title="Russian Empire">Russian Empire</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1829" title="1829">1829</a> – <a href="/wiki/Charles_Fremantle" title="Charles Fremantle">Charles Fremantle</a> arrives in HMS Challenger off the coast of modern-day Western Australia prior to declaring the <a href="/wiki/Swan_River_Colony" title="Swan River Colony">Swan River Colony</a> for the United Kingdom.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1846" title="1846">1846</a> – <a href="/wiki/Thornton_Affair" title="Thornton Affair">Thornton Affair</a>: Open conflict begins over the disputed border of <a href="/wiki/Texas" title="Texas">Texas</a>, triggering the <a href="/wiki/Mexican%E2%80%93American_War" title="Mexican–American War">Mexican–American War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1847" title="1847">1847</a> – The last survivors of the <a href="/wiki/Donner_Party" title="Donner Party">Donner Party</a> are out of the wilderness.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1849" title="1849">1849</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Governor_General_of_Canada" title="Governor General of Canada">Governor General of Canada</a>, <a href="/wiki/James_Bruce,_8th_Earl_of_Elgin" title="James Bruce, 8th Earl of Elgin">Lord Elgin</a>, signs the <a href="/wiki/Rebellion_Losses_Bill" title="Rebellion Losses Bill">Rebellion Losses Bill</a>, outraging <a href="/wiki/Montreal" title="Montreal">Montreal</a>'s <a href="/wiki/English_Canadian" title="English Canadian">English</a> population and triggering the <a href="/wiki/Montreal_Riots" class="mw-redirect" title="Montreal Riots">Montreal Riots</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1859" title="1859">1859</a> – British and French engineers break ground for the <a href="/wiki/Suez_Canal" title="Suez Canal">Suez Canal</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1862" title="1862">1862</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Civil_War" title="American Civil War">American Civil War</a>: Forces under <a href="/wiki/Union_(American_Civil_War)" title="Union (American Civil War)">Union</a> <a href="/wiki/Admiral" title="Admiral">Admiral</a> <a href="/wiki/David_Farragut" title="David Farragut">David Farragut</a> <a href="/wiki/Capture_of_New_Orleans" title="Capture of New Orleans">demand the surrender</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Confederate_States_of_America" title="Confederate States of America">Confederate</a> city of <a href="/wiki/New_Orleans" title="New Orleans">New Orleans, Louisiana</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1864" title="1864">1864</a> – American Civil War: The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Marks%27_Mills" title="Battle of Marks' Mills">Battle of Marks' Mills</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1882" title="1882">1882</a> – <a href="/wiki/Tonkin_Campaign" title="Tonkin Campaign">Tonkin Campaign</a>: <a href="/wiki/French_Third_Republic" title="French Third Republic">French</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nguy%E1%BB%85n_dynasty" title="Nguyễn dynasty">Vietnamese</a> troops clashed in <a href="/wiki/Tonkin" title="Tonkin">Tonkin</a>, when Commandant <a href="/wiki/Henri_Rivi%C3%A8re_(naval_officer)" title="Henri Rivière (naval officer)">Henri Rivière</a> <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hanoi_(1882)" title="Battle of Hanoi (1882)">seized</a> the <a href="/wiki/Citadel_of_Hanoi" class="mw-redirect" title="Citadel of Hanoi">citadel of Hanoi</a> with a small force of marine infantry.</li>

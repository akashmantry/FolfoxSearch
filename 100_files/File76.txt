<p><b>April 23</b> is the 113th day of the year (114th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 252 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Monday, Thursday or Saturday (58 in 400 years each) than on Tuesday or Wednesday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Friday or Sunday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=April_23&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/215_BC" title="215 BC">215 BC</a> – <a href="/wiki/Temple_of_Venus_Erycina" title="Temple of Venus Erycina">A temple</a> is built on the <a href="/wiki/Capitoline_Hill" title="Capitoline Hill">Capitoline Hill</a> dedicated to <a href="/wiki/Venus_Erycina" class="mw-redirect" title="Venus Erycina">Venus Erycina</a> to commemorate the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Republic" title="Roman Republic">Roman</a> defeat at <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Lake_Trasimene" title="Battle of Lake Trasimene">Lake Trasimene</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/599" title="599">599</a> – Maya king <a href="/wiki/Scroll_Serpent" title="Scroll Serpent">Uneh Chan</a> of <a href="/wiki/Calakmul" title="Calakmul">Calakmul</a> attacks rival city-state <a href="/wiki/Palenque" title="Palenque">Palenque</a> in southern Mexico, defeating queen <a href="/wiki/Yohl_Ik%27nal" title="Yohl Ik'nal">Yohl Ik'nal</a> and sacking the city.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/711" title="711">711</a> – <a href="/wiki/Dagobert_III" title="Dagobert III">Dagobert III</a> is crowned <a href="/wiki/King_of_the_Franks" class="mw-redirect" title="King of the Franks">King of the Franks</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1014" title="1014">1014</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Clontarf" title="Battle of Clontarf">Battle of Clontarf</a>: High King of Ireland <a href="/wiki/Brian_Boru" title="Brian Boru">Brian Boru</a> defeats <a href="/wiki/Viking" class="mw-redirect" title="Viking">Viking</a> invaders, but is killed in battle.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1016" title="1016">1016</a> – <a href="/wiki/Edmund_Ironside" title="Edmund Ironside">Edmund Ironside</a> succeeds his father <a href="/wiki/%C3%86thelred_the_Unready" title="Æthelred the Unready">Æthelred the Unready</a> as king of England.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1343" title="1343">1343</a> – <a href="/wiki/St._George%27s_Night_Uprising" class="mw-redirect" title="St. George's Night Uprising">St. George's Night Uprising</a> commences in the <a href="/wiki/Danish_Estonia#Duchy_of_Estonia" title="Danish Estonia">Duchy of Estonia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1348" title="1348">1348</a> – The founding of the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Garter" title="Order of the Garter">Order of the Garter</a> by King <a href="/wiki/Edward_III_of_England" title="Edward III of England">Edward III</a> is announced on <a href="/wiki/Saint_George" title="Saint George">St. George's Day</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1516" title="1516">1516</a> – The Bayerische <a href="/wiki/Reinheitsgebot" title="Reinheitsgebot">Reinheitsgebot</a> (regarding the ingredients of beer) is signed in <a href="/wiki/Ingolstadt" title="Ingolstadt">Ingolstadt</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1521" title="1521">1521</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Villalar" title="Battle of Villalar">Battle of Villalar</a>: <a href="/wiki/Charles_V,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor">King Charles I of Spain</a> defeats the <a href="/wiki/Revolt_of_the_Comuneros" title="Revolt of the Comuneros">Comuneros</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1635" title="1635">1635</a> – The first public school in the United States, <a href="/wiki/Boston_Latin_School" title="Boston Latin School">Boston Latin School</a>, is founded in <a href="/wiki/Boston" title="Boston">Boston</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1655" title="1655">1655</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Santo_Domingo_(1655)" title="Siege of Santo Domingo (1655)">Siege of Santo Domingo</a> begins during the <a href="/wiki/Anglo-Spanish_War_(1654%E2%80%9360)" class="mw-redirect" title="Anglo-Spanish War (1654–60)">Anglo-Spanish War</a>, and fails seven days later.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1660" title="1660">1660</a> – <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Oliva" title="Treaty of Oliva">Treaty of Oliva</a> is established between Sweden and Poland.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1661" title="1661">1661</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Charles_II_of_England" title="Charles II of England">Charles II of England</a>, Scotland and Ireland is crowned in <a href="/wiki/Westminster_Abbey" title="Westminster Abbey">Westminster Abbey</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1815" title="1815">1815</a> – <a href="/wiki/Second_Serbian_Uprising" title="Second Serbian Uprising">The Second Serbian Uprising</a>: A second phase of the national revolution of the Serbs against the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a>, erupts shortly after the annexation of the country to the Ottoman Empire.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1879" title="1879">1879</a> – Fire burns down the second main building and dome of the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Notre_Dame" title="University of Notre Dame">University of Notre Dame</a>, which prompts the construction of the third, and current, <a href="/wiki/Main_Building_(University_of_Notre_Dame)" title="Main Building (University of Notre Dame)">Main Building</a> with its golden dome.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1914" title="1914">1914</a> – First <a href="/wiki/Baseball" title="Baseball">baseball</a> game at <a href="/wiki/History_of_Wrigley_Field" title="History of Wrigley Field">Wrigley Field</a>, then known as Weeghman Park, in Chicago.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1918" title="1918">1918</a> – World War I: The <a href="/wiki/Royal_Navy" title="Royal Navy">British Royal Navy</a> makes a <a href="/wiki/Zeebrugge_Raid" title="Zeebrugge Raid">raid</a> in an attempt to neutralise the Belgian <a href="/wiki/Port_of_Bruges-Zeebrugge" class="mw-redirect" title="Port of Bruges-Zeebrugge">port of Bruges-Zeebrugge</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1920" title="1920">1920</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Grand_National_Assembly_of_Turkey" title="Grand National Assembly of Turkey">Grand National Assembly of Turkey</a> (TBMM) is founded in <a href="/wiki/Ankara" title="Ankara">Ankara</a>. The assembly denounces the government of <a href="/wiki/Sultan" title="Sultan">Sultan</a> <a href="/wiki/Mehmed_VI" title="Mehmed VI">Mehmed VI</a> and announces the preparation of a <a href="/wiki/Turkish_Constitution_of_1921" title="Turkish Constitution of 1921">temporary constitution</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a> – <a href="/wiki/Cardiff_City_F.C." title="Cardiff City F.C.">Cardiff City</a> defeat <a href="/wiki/Arsenal_F.C." title="Arsenal F.C.">Arsenal</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1927_FA_Cup_Final" title="1927 FA Cup Final">FA Cup Final</a>, the only time it has been won by a team not based in England.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1935" title="1935">1935</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Polish_Constitution_of_1935" class="mw-redirect" title="Polish Constitution of 1935">Polish Constitution of 1935</a> is adopted.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1940" title="1940">1940</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Rhythm_Club_fire" title="Rhythm Club fire">Rhythm Club fire</a> at a dance hall in <a href="/wiki/Natchez,_Mississippi" title="Natchez, Mississippi">Natchez, Mississippi</a>, kills 198 people.</li>
